18:14:43 From Ravi Rau to Everyone:
	A caution regarding slide 23. Given an energy spectrum with say a very large number N of levels, the Darboux construction can make another potential with all but just one energy level preserved, that is, remove just one energy level. If N very large, it will be difficult to distinguish the two spectra  on the basis of some subset known. Note also that knowing just energy not enough because the two phase-equivalent potentials may have different wavefunctions even while having energy values same.    Ravi Rau.  ravirau@lsu.edu
